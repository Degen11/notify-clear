Concept

Badge Buster

One tap to wipe every red dot off your home screen. A clean slate for your phone, and your head.

The idea

You know that creeping anxiety when your home screen is littered with red notification badges? 12 unread emails, 99+ messages, 5 app updates you'll never install. Badge Buster is built around a dead-simple premise — hit one button and make all of that disappear.

Why it's a concept

Here's the catch: iOS and Android sandbox every app for security and privacy. No app can reach into another app's notification badges and clear them. It's just not how mobile operating systems work. So Badge Buster will never be a real product — it's a design exploration of what the UX could feel like if that restriction didn't exist.

The frustration it reflects

Badge fatigue is real. Those little red circles are designed to pull you back into apps, and most of the time they're noise, not signal. Badge Buster is a nod to the shared desire for a calmer, less demanding phone experience.

Built by Degen Hill  ·  A UX concept, not a shipping product.
